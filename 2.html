How It Works

Our revolutionary technology creates a seamless bridge between the digital and physical worlds, enabling natural interaction with virtual objects in your environment.

①

Environment Mapping

Our AI scans and understands your space in real-time, creating a detailed 3D map that recognizes surfaces, objects, and spatial relationships with millimeter precision.

②

Virtual Object Placement

Place digital objects into your physical environment with realistic physics, lighting, and shadows. They appear as if they truly exist in your space.

③

Use intuitive hand gestures to manipulate virtual objects - rotate, scale, move, and interact with them as if they were physically present. No controllers needed.

Advanced Technology

Our proprietary technology stack combines cutting-edge AI with spatial computing to deliver unparalleled experiences.

Neural Hand Tracking

Our AI models track hand movements with sub-millimeter precision, understanding complex gestures and enabling natural interaction with virtual objects.

The system continuously updates as the environment changes, maintaining accurate placement of virtual objects even as people and objects move through the space.
